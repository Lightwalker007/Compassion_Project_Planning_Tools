The Compassion Project: Evangelism Planning Kit

This kit contains two essential tools for coordinating your upcoming evangelism event.

1. Evangelism Planning Tool (Template)
	File: Evangelism_Planning_Tool_Template.html
	Purpose: Use this to build your daily schedule, track giveaways, meal plans, and manage your volunteer roster. 
	Usage: Open the file, set your start date and duration, and click "Generate Schedule".

2. Evangelism Meeting Outline Guide
	File: Evangelism_Meeting_Outline_Guide.html
	Purpose: A step-by-step guide for the 5 planning meetings. It includes a dynamic "Capacity 	Grid" (Matrix) to track volunteer coverage across all teams and dates.
	Usage: Open the file, enter your dates, and click "Build Matrix".

---

 Instructions for Use:
1. Unzip this folder to your computer.
2. Double-click either HTML file to open it in your web browser.
3. Internet Access: You must be connected to the internet for the styles and fonts to load correctly.
4. Saving your work: Click the "Save Progress" button at the top of the tool. This will download a NEW version of the file with all your data saved inside it. You can open that new file later to continue editing.
5. Exporting: Click "Export to PDF" to generate a clean, print-ready document for your records.
